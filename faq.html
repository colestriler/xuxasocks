What can I use custom socks for?
Custom socks can be extremeley valuable to any organization. Schools & teams use them as successful fundraisers, modern businesses use them for marketing, and some people just like always having new socks.
How much can I resell the socks for?
We design our pricing so that you can double your money. Our socks have a MSRP value of $12-15.
How much do custom socks cost?
You can find all of our pricing on our pricing page. If you don't find what you're looking for, feel free to contact us!
How long does production take?
Production times vary depending on the size of the order and the time of the year. Typically, for orders between 300-600 pairs, production takes about four weeks after the order is placed. Once production is finished, it takes about another week to ship the socks to the US.
What size are the socks?
Our standard socks are designed to fit male shoe sizes 8-12 , and we can customize different sizes as well.
