Business Development Intern

  1. Home
  2. Internships

Goal: Sell socks to different campus departments for fundraisers, retail, or promotion (i.e. sports teams, greek life, clubs).

College interns are very important to us. We look for students who are confident and hard working. Interns use their campus network to meet with campus departments who need fundraising ideas or products to sell. They collect custom sock orders and earn a 50% commission on the profits of their sales. We send interns socks to wear and socks to give campus departments as samples.
